# Why Fintech, Lending, and Senior Leadership Roles

## Why This Direction Fits
Justin is drawn to fintech, lending, and adjacent leadership roles because they sit at the intersection of several things he does well:
- analytical problem solving
- operating judgment
- risk-balanced growth thinking
- cross-functional leadership
- building systems that influence real financial outcomes

These roles are appealing because they require more than technical competence. They reward leaders who can combine customer understanding, economics, regulation-aware thinking, and scalable execution.

## Why Lending and Risk Work Is Interesting
Justin is especially interested in lending and risk work because it involves meaningful tradeoffs with real-world consequences. Decisions affect customers, business performance, and long-term portfolio quality all at once. That complexity creates room for thoughtful leadership and differentiated judgment.

He enjoys work where success depends on:
- measuring the right thing
- making tradeoffs explicit
- resisting shallow optimization
- building mechanisms that scale
- helping organizations act on evidence rather than instinct alone

## Why Senior Leadership
Justin is best positioned for senior leadership roles because his value is not limited to individual analysis. He has experience helping define direction, shape decision frameworks, improve organizational capability, and align diverse stakeholders around difficult choices.

He is particularly effective when a company needs someone who can:
- lead teams directly
- influence beyond formal authority
- improve how decisions get made
- connect technical work to enterprise outcomes
- raise both execution quality and organizational clarity

## What He Is Looking For
Justin is most interested in roles where he can have outsized impact on:
- strategy and decision quality
- team building and leadership development
- responsible growth
- scalable risk and lending systems
- strong analytical and operating mechanisms

## Employer-Facing Answer
A concise answer to "Why are you interested in fintech or lending leadership roles?" could be:

Justin is drawn to fintech and lending leadership because the work combines rigorous decision-making, meaningful customer and economic impact, and the kind of cross-functional complexity where he does his best work. He enjoys environments where the challenge is not just to grow, but to grow responsibly and with strong systems behind the decisions.

## Why He May Be Looking Beyond a Current Role
A strong public-safe framing is that Justin is motivated by opportunities where he can apply his experience in risk, lending, and data science to a mission, platform, or leadership scope that is especially well matched to his strengths. He is excited by roles that allow him to shape both outcomes and the organizational capability behind those outcomes.
